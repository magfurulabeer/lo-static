Raising the Bar Since 1983

Legal Outreach seeks to level the educational playing field for minority, low income,
and first-generation students by implementing programs designed to fill the
achievement, outlook, and matching gaps which prevent students from achieving their
highest aspirations and reaching their professional goals.

Achievement Gap

Helping students develop the skills needed to excel academically in high school, college, and beyond.

Outlook Gap

Raising students' awareness of professional opportunities and lifting their aspirations.

Matching Gap

Matching students to the correct set of colleges based on their academic performance, needs, and aspirations.
